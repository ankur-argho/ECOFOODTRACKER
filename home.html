Welcome to ECOFOODTRACKER

ECOFOODTRACKER is an innovative online platform that helps you calculate the environmental impact of your food choices. Our mission is to empower individuals to make sustainable food choices that reduce their carbon footprint and contribute to a healthier planet.

How it Works,

  1. Select Your Location: Choose your state or region to access localized food choices and environmental impact data.
  2. Choose Your Food: Select from a variety of food options, including vegetarian, non-vegetarian, and vegan choices.
  3. Calculate Your Impact: Get an instant estimate of the environmental impact of your food choices, including water usage, greenhouse gas emissions, and land use.

Key Features,

  • Indian States Map: Explore food choices and environmental impact data across 28 Indian states.
  • Food Impact Calculator: Calculate the environmental impact of your food choices.
  • Sustainable Food Options: Discover eco-friendly food alternatives and reduce your carbon footprint.

Benefits

  • Reduce Your Carbon Footprint: Make informed food choices that minimize harm to the environment.
  • Contribute to a Healthier Planet: Join a community of individuals working towards a sustainable future.
  • Make Informed Choices: Access reliable data and insights to inform your food choices
